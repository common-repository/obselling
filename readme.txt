== Description ==

**WooCommerce Upselling and Cross-selling plugin of obsolete or out of stock products.**

Don't delete your old products anymore, sell more!

Sell other products to buy if your product is no longer on sale, or out of stock, without losing your ranking in Google!

= 4 advantages =

**Selling**
Suggest up to 4 others products to replace your obsolete product.

**SEO Google**
Your obsolete product is always referenced by Google!

**Redirection**
No sudden redirection to another product or another page.

**Deactivation**
Your product is present in your store but not available for purchase.

= FREE version =

* Make a product obsolete
* Make it impossible to add the obsolete product to the cart
* Hide: Price
* Hide: Add to cart
* Hide: Product quantity
* Hide: Product variation
* Number of deactivatable products (obsolete): *3*
* Number of recommended products: *1*

= PRO version =

All features FREE version.

* Number of deactivatable products (obsolete): *Unlimited*
* Number of recommended products: *4*
* Add recommended products if the product is *out of stock*
* Shortcode (Divi, Elementor,…)
* Product display order (Name / Price)
* Message customization
* Hide: In Lists / Views

Get PRO version: [Obselling EN](https://obselling.com/) | [Obselling FR](https://obselling.fr/)

== Installation FREE VERSION ==

* Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/obselling` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
* Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.

== Installation PRO VERSION ==

*You must FREE version to use the PRO version.*

* Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/obselling-pro` directory
* Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
* Use the Settings->Obselling PRO to configure the plugin and add your licence key for all features.

Get a complete [Obselling EN](https://obselling.com/ "Obselling") | [Obselling FR](https://obselling.fr/ "Obselling") for PRO version.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What does Obselling mean? =

Is the fusion of the word *obsolete* and the word *selling*.

= What does Obselling do, exactly? =

Obselling will allow you to stop deleting products from your store and to give the possibility to propose other replacement products.

= What is the benefit for SEO and Google? =

You no longer delete your product pages already referenced by Google, so you keep the authority of the product page and you offer others to your customers.

= What’s the difference between FREE and PRO version? =

The FREE version is only limited to the number of recommended products and features.
